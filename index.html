Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kinder or kindergarten is the first stage of education in a child's life. At this age, children begin to learn new things, such as letters, numbers, colors, and different shapes. Their learning method is usually through play, which helps them develop their creativity and imagination.

Elementary School

Elementary school is the second stage of a child's educational life, where they gain basic understanding of various subjects. At this stage, they acquire basic knowledge of various subjects, including mathematics, language, science, and social studies.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school or secondary school is a very important time in a child's life, where they enter adolescence. At this age, they undergo physical and mental changes. During this time, they begin to learn more complex subjects, such as advanced mathematics, science, literature, and history.
